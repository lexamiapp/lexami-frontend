# Community Space - User Guide

## Interacting with Posts

Your Nyay Mitra community spaces now support full social media-style interactions, similar to LinkedIn. Here's how to use each feature:

### 👍 Like
**Purpose**: Show appreciation for a post

**How to use**:
1. Tap the "Like" button below any post
2. The button turns blue when you've liked it
3. Tap again to unlike

**What happens**:
- Your like is counted and displayed
- The post author may receive a notification
- The button shows your active state in blue

---

### 💬 Comment
**Purpose**: Start a conversation or share your thoughts

**How to use**:
1. Tap the "Comment" button
2. A bottom sheet opens with existing comments
3. Type your comment in the text field
4. Tap the send icon to post

**Features**:
- Comments appear in chronological order
- Your display name is shown (or "Anonymous" if your space is hidden)
- Comments are visible to all space followers

---

### 🔄 Repost
**Purpose**: Share valuable content with your followers

**How to use**:
1. Tap the "Repost" button
2. A confirmation dialog appears
3. Tap "Repost" to confirm

**Requirements**:
- You must have created your own Space first
- You can only repost each post once

**What happens**:
- The post appears in your Space feed
- Original author attribution is maintained
- Shows "You reposted" at the top
- Your followers can see the reposted content

**To undo a repost**:
1. Tap the "Repost" button again (it will be green)
2. The repost is automatically removed

---

### 📤 Send/Share
**Purpose**: Share posts outside the app

**How to use**:
1. Tap the "Send" button
2. Post link is automatically copied to clipboard
3. Paste the link anywhere (WhatsApp, email, SMS, etc.)

**What's copied**:
```
[Post content]

Shared from [Author Name] on Nyay Mitra
https://nyaymitra.in/space/@[handle]/post/[id]
```

---

## Understanding Post Stats

Above the interaction buttons, you'll see engagement statistics:

- **👍 24** - Number of likes
- **12 comments** - Number of comments
- **5 reposts** - Number of times the post was reposted

These stats help you identify popular and valuable content in the community.

---

## Reposted Content

When you see a repost, it will show:

1. **Top banner**: "🔄 [Space Name] reposted" in gray
2. **Original author**: The original creator's name and profile picture
3. **"Original Post" label**: Indicates this is reposted content

This ensures proper attribution and helps you discover both the reposter and the original creator.

## Best Practices

### When to Like
- Content you find helpful or informative
- Posts that answer your questions
- Quality legal advice or insights

### When to Comment
- You have a follow-up question
- You want to share your experience
- You have additional insights to add

### When to Repost
- Valuable legal information your followers should see
- Important updates or announcements
- Educational content worth spreading

### When to Share
- You want to share with someone outside the app
- You need to reference the post later
- You're discussing the content on another platform

---

## Privacy Notes

- **Anonymous commenting**: If your Space is set to "Hidden", your comments will show as "Anonymous"
- **Repost visibility**: Reposts appear in your Space, so they're visible to your followers
- **Like privacy**: Likes are counted but individual likers are not publicly displayed
- **Share tracking**: The app doesn't track where you paste shared links

---

## Tips for Engagement

1. **Be respectful**: Keep discussions professional and constructive
2. **Add value**: When commenting or reposting, consider adding your perspective
3. **Give credit**: Reposts automatically credit the original author
4. **Stay relevant**: Interact with content related to your legal interests
5. **Build connections**: Engaging with posts helps you connect with legal experts and peers

---

## Troubleshooting

**Can't repost?**
- Make sure you've created your own Space first
- You may have already reposted this content

**Comments not showing?**
- Check your internet connection
- Pull down to refresh the feed

**Share link not working?**
- The link format is designed for future web access
- For now, use it to share the post content
