Alimony in India:
Under Section 25 of the Hindu Marriage Act, the court can order the husband or wife to pay alimony.
Factors considered:
1. Income and assets of both parties.
2. Conduct of both parties.
3. Standard of living during the marriage.
4. Duration of the marriage.
5. Sacrifices made by one spouse for the other's career.

Types of Alimony:
- Interim Alimony: Paid during the litigation process.
- Permanent Alimony: Paid after the divorce decree.
