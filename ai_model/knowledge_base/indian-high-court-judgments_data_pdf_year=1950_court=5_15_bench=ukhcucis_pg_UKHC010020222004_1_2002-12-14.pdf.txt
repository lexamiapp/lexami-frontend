WPMB No. 886/2002 
 
 
Hon. P.C. Verma, J. 
Hon'ble M.M. Ghildiyal, J.
 
 
  Heard Sri Lok Pal Singh  
learned counsel for the petitioner 
and the learned Standing Counsel. 
 
 This petition has been filed 
by the petitioner seeking a writ of 
certiorari to quash the FIR dated 
8.12.2002 contained in annexure 1 
to the writ petition. A perusal of the 
FIR shows that it contains the 
offence alleged to have been 
committed by the petitioner under 
various sections of the I.P.C. The 
investigation is going on. Since the 
investigation is going on, therefore, 
we are not inclined to entertain this 
petition. 
 The petitioner shall appear on 
every day before the Investigating 
Officer and the Investigating  
Officer shall conduct the inquiry   
day to day till the inquiry is 
completed. H owever the petitioner 
shall not be taken into custody 
during the night. Till the credible 
material is not found with the I.O. 
the petitioner shall not be taken into 
custody. 
 The petition is dismissed  
with the above observations. 
   
 
 
 (M.M. Ghildiyal, J.) (P.C. Verma, J.)   
 
14.12.2002
